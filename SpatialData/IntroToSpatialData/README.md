These worksheets are taken from the undergraduate course 'Data Science for Ecologists & Environmental Scientists'. There may be a few contextual cues that don't make total sense but otherwise these should mostly stand alone. 

First have a scroll through the slides (you'll may need to refer to other material to help you understand these)
Then open the PDF worksheet and the R script. Work through these, and avoid opening the 'ANSWERS' script until you're done (or REALLY stuck, but solving problems is how you learn, so don't be tempted until you've tried for a good long while)
